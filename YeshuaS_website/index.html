Top Ten Pies for the Holidays

These are, in my opinion, the top ten best pies for the holidays. these pies are sure to make your guest very happy!!!

10. Chart Pies

These pies are by far the worse ones on this list. They are not even edible! yuck

9. Key Lime Pie

Don't get me wrong, this is one solid pie right here. It is rich and creamy. It's the fruit that's the problem. Lime is not the best choice forsay.

8. Lemon Meringue Pie

It's for the same reasons as the Key Lime Pie. The only reason it ranks higher is because the tartness is more pronounced in this delectable delight.

7. Cherry Pie

An absolute classic this one! Ranks somewhat low because it's a basic, good'old reliable flavor. Only problem is that there are better flavors to choose from.

6. Blueberry Pie

A truly delectable treat is the clasic Blueberry Pie. It places here on the list because of the same reasons as the Cherry Pie. Ranks higher though because of it's more light and sweet flavor.

5. Beef Pot Pie

Now this is a pie if I ever saw one. Breaks away from the norm of sweet pies by being rich and savory. And man, does it pay off! Who knew beef tasted so good with a pie crust.

4. Pumpkin Pie

Now, I know what you must be thinking. But trust me, this pie taste amazing anytime of year. Whether it's at a Thanksgiving table with the fam, or you just had a craving, the Pumpkin Pie is an excellent choice all the time.

3. Peacan Pie

Ok so hear me out: I've never actually eaten this pie before. But, my brother did say it tasted really REALLY good, so I'll take his word on it.

2. Apple Pie

Ah yes, the good'old Apple Pie. You can never go wrong with it. Just about everyone has tasted this simply incredible pie, with it's wonderful flavor, filled with so much ... wonder! Now you might be just a bit surpised that it's not on the number one spot, but you'll see real soon why that's that case. Got a real special one coming for yall next.

AND FINALLY, NUMBER 1:

Chicken Pot Pie

This, right here, is, in my humble opinion, the greatest pie that has ever grazed the earth itself. What an absolute experiance this pie is. Taking the best parts of a pie, and then making it into a full, hearty and filling, meal. Absolutly delicious the Chicken Pot Pie is. If you have not have had the incredible pleasure of consuming one of these pies, do yourself a favor, and give it a try. You will NOT regret it!
